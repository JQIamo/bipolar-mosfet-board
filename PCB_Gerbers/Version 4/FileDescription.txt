bipolarMOSFETv4A.zip:	Zip-archive containing the following Gerber files for generating boards:
	bipolarMosfetv4.cmp:	Component-side copper layer (Layer 1 of 4)
	bipolarMosfetv4.drd:	Excellon NC drill file (drill hit and size info)
	bipolarMosfetv4.dri:	Drill-station info file
	bipolarMosfetv4.l15:	Inner copper signal layer, positive polarity (Layer 3 of 4)
	bipolarMosfetv4.ly2:	Inner copper signal layer, positive polarity (Layer 2 of 4)
	bipolarMosfetv4.plc:	Component-side silkscreen
	bipolarMosfetv4.sol:	Solder-side copper layer (Layer 4 of 4)
	bipolarMosfetv4.stc:	Component-side solder mask
	bipolarMosfetv4.sts:	Solder-side solder mask
